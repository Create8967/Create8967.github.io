Quick water info

People use water every day for many different things. 3% of water is fresh water and the other 97% is salt water. Water is also used by animals and plants

Water Pollution

About water pollution

Water pollution is bad. Almost 60% of species live in water. Meaning if all the water was polluted, Everything would end up dieing.

Cause and effect of water pollution

Water pollution is caused by different things. One thing is because of the industrial wastes dumped into the rivers and other bodies of water. This will cause an imbalance in the water leading to severe contamination and death of aquatic species. Including fish which people eat so it will end up killing more than aquatic species.

Another thing is spraying insecticides and pesticides on plants because it pollutes the ground water system. A third thing is oil spills in the oceans. They cause irreparable damage to the water bodies.

Water Footprint

What is a water footprint

A water footprint shows the extent of water use in relation to consumption by people.(https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Water_footprint)

Easy things

If you want to start helping but don't know what, here is a list of easy tasks you can do to save water.

  • Don't leave tap on while brushing teeth
  • Take showers instead of baths but don't make it a long shower.
  • Don't always buy new clothes, you can try buying used ones
  • Compost your eaten fruits and vegtables. Don't through them in the garbage.

You have probably heard about some of these things before.

You can do some research if you would want to do more.
